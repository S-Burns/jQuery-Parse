$.parse

A super light-weight AJAX wrapper for Parse.com's wonderful database service.

Try out this demo

  1. Check out the docs..
  2. In your Parse.com account, create a app named "demo"
  3. Pass your "demo" application key & rest key to $.parse.init in demo.js (line 7-8)
  4. Try out this task list app by creating, deleting & editing tasks.
